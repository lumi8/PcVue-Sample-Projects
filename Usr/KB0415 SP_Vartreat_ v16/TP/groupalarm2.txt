TREATMENT,ACKALARMBYVAR,Ack by area,0,,,,,,,1,10,#F=(#A2==LEVEL),#B1.TANK.LEVEL.ACK,1

Alarm variables with a nature of LEVEL and a priority between 1 and 10 are affected.

AREA1.TANK.LEVEL.ACK will acknowledge alarms with the pattern AREA1.*
AREA2.TANK.LEVEL.ACK will acknowledge alarms with the pattern AREA2.*
