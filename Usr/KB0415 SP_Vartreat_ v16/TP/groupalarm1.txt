TREATMENT,ACKALARMBYVAR,Ack by tank,0,,,,,,,1,10,#F=(#A2==LEVEL),#B1.#B2.LEVEL.ACK,1

Alarm variables with a domain of LEVEL and a priority between 1 and 10 are affected.

AREA1.TANK1.LEVEL.ACK will acknowledge alarms with the pattern AREA1.TANK1.*
AREA1.TANK2.LEVEL.ACK will acknowledge alarms with the pattern AREA1.TANK2.*
